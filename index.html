The Water Crisis is Solvable

Over 2 billion people lack access to safe water, resulting in preventable deaths and suffering. But evidence shows us exactly how to fix this.

⚠️

1+ Million Deaths Annually

Contaminated water causes over one million preventable deaths each year from diseases like typhoid, cholera, and hepatitis A.

👶

Children Most Affected

Diarrhea from unsafe water is the second-leading cause of death in children under five years old worldwide.

🌍

Global Scale Challenge

Rural communities in Sub-Saharan Africa face the greatest challenges, often walking hours for water that still isn't safe to drink.

Our Evidence-Based Solutions

We use proven, scalable technologies to deliver safe water where it's needed most.

Chlorine Dispensers for Rural Communities

Our bright blue dispensers are installed directly at water sources across Kenya, Malawi, and Uganda. Community members simply turn a valve to get the correct chlorine dose for their water container.

  • Automatic correct dosing - no measuring required
  • Free for communities to use
  • Disinfects water during the walk home
  • WHO-endorsed chlorination technology
  • Maintained by local circuit riders

How It Works

Turn valve → Chlorine drops in bucket → Fill with water → Walk home → Safe water ready to drink

Automatic In-Line Chlorination

🏘️ Installed on communal water systems

⚡ Automatic treatment as water flows

🔧 No user action required

In-Line Chlorination for Piped Systems

In peri-urban and urban communities with communal piped water systems, we install devices directly on water tanks for automatic chlorination.

  • Automatic treatment - no behavior change needed
  • Treats water as it flows through pipes
  • Safe water delivered directly to homes
  • Covers entire community water systems
  • Cost-effective for dense populations

How We Ensure Lasting Impact

1

Community Partnership

We engage local leaders and community members before installation, ensuring buy-in and selecting volunteer promoters.

2

Strategic Installation

Dispensers are placed right next to water sources, making safe water practices easy to adopt and remember.

3

Last-Mile Service

Our circuit riders visit regularly to maintain dispensers, refill chlorine, and address issues within 72 hours.

4

Continuous Monitoring

We test household water monthly for chlorine residue - the true measure of whether families have safe drinking water.
